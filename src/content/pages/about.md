I'm **Naman Gupta**, a senior backend and platform engineer with a focus on building reliable, scalable systems.

This blog is where I write about the technical challenges I encounter and the solutions I design. Topics include:

- **System Design** — Distributed systems, event-driven architecture, and high-throughput services
- **FinTech** — Payment processing, ledger systems, and financial infrastructure
- **Cloud & Infrastructure** — Kubernetes, Terraform, and platform engineering
- **AI & ML** — Practical applications of AI in engineering workflows

## What to Expect

Posts here are technical deep-dives — not hot takes or surface-level tutorials. I write about real production systems, the trade-offs involved in architectural decisions, and lessons learned from operating services at scale.
